Korean Sweet Potato

An illegal mix of sweet potato and honey on pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 Sweet Potato
  • A good amount of Salami
  • 1 Onion
  • Sliced Mushrooms
  • Shredded Mozarella Cheese
  • Mozarella Ball
  • Honey
  • Hot Chilli Flakes
  • Pizza Sauce

Steps

Preparing the Ingredients

  1. Peel and Chop the Sweet Potato and then boil.
  2. Once boiled mash and mix in with honey until sweet.
  3. Slice Onion.

Making the pizza base

  1. Follow instructions on this website to make a pizza base.

Cooking the Pizza

  1. With your pizza base spread some pizza sauce.
  2. Add a layer of mozzarella.
  3. Add onions, mushrooms and salami.
  4. Using an icing tube place dollops of sweet potato on the pizza
  5. Rip some mozzarella ball and place on pizza.
  6. Put in oven at highest temperature for 10-15mins.
