Babi Kecap

Babi Kecap is Indonesian style Braised Pork

Ingredients

  • A good amount of Pork Belly
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Firm Tofu at least 450 grams
  • 4 Eggs
  • Steamed Rice
  • Kecap Manis (Sweet Soy Sauce)

Steps

Preparing the Ingredients

  1. Chop the pork belly roughly into 1cm cubes.
  2. Boil eggs for 10 mins to get hard-boiled eggs.
  3. Blanch pork belly to take out scum.
  4. Slice ginger into thin sticks.
  5. Finely chop garlic.

Cooking the Babi Kecap

  1. Heat up a wok to medium heat and the add oil and ginger and garlic.
  2. Stir garlic and ginger until they start to brown then add pork belly.
  3. Once the pork belly browns pour in some kecap manis and oyster sauce to taste.
  4. Once the sauce is to your liking add in a corn flour slurry to thicken the broth.

Serving the Babi Kecap

  1. Serve on top or with rice.
